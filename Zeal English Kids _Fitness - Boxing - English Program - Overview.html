Session Structure (55 minutes)

  • Small Talk & Vocabulary Introduction (5 min)
  • Warm-up Game (8 min)
  • Skill Circuit A (12 min)
  • Skill Circuit B (12 min)
  • Boxing Skills Block (10 min)
  • Cool-down & Recovery (5 min)
  • Exit Ticket Assessment (30 sec - 1 min)

Age Band Differentiation

  • Band A (Grades 1-3): Shorter distances, 10-15 sec efforts
  • Simplified movements, 3-5 quality repetitions
  • Single-word commands, gesture support
  • Band B (Grades 4-6): Longer distances, 15-20 sec efforts
  • Complex combinations, 6-8 repetitions
  • Multi-step instructions, sentence production

Vocabulary Progression

  • Sessions 1-4: Basic commands, body parts, directions
  • Sessions 5-8: Descriptive adjectives, quality words
  • Sessions 9-12: Team language, self-assessment terms
  • Total: 120+ vocabulary words over 12 sessions
  • 10 target words per session with physical anchoring

Safety & Scalability

  • All boxing elements are non-contact
  • Soft equipment for defensive drills
  • Works for 1 child (trainer participation) or 6 children
  • Each session functions independently for irregular attendance
  • Progressive difficulty with monthly assessment checkpoints
